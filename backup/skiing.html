Cross-country — Jønnbu / lowland tracks

Tracks are free to use. Consider donating to the local trail orgs when grooming is active.

Rentals & lessons

  • Alpine: enquire at Lifjell base for skis/boards/helmets.
  • XC: some shops in Bø carry classic/BC gear seasonally.
  • Group lessons: often arranged weekends/holidays at the resort.
  • Respect closures and grooming; keep dogs under control.
  • Tip: reserve during busy school holidays.

Winter safety

  • Dress in layers; protect fingers, toes, head and face from windchill.
  • Check visibility and wind before choosing routes.
  • Carry a light, map/app with offline coverage, and a small repair kit.
  • If you have a Recco chip feel free to use it.
  • Always bring more food and water than you expect you need.
  • Be careful of snow blindness! Bring sunglasses or similar with color.
