Campsite finder (Bø i Telemark)

Wild camping (allemannsretten)

  • Pitch 150 m+ from the nearest inhabited house/cabin.
  • Max 2 nights in the same spot (longer in high mountains/remote areas).
  • Stay off cultivated land; leave no trace; respect grazing and nesting.

Rules shown here are summaries; always follow local signs and guidance.

Campfire & stove safety

  • General ban 15 Apr–15 Sep on open fires in/near forest & uncultivated land.
  • Outside that period: only when safe (no wind / drought; clear to mineral soil).
  • Disposable grills count as fires; use official fire sites when available.

Check local municipal notices before lighting any fire.

Tips for a great night out

  1. Pick a sheltered spot and respect local guidelines.
  2. Bring layers, headlamp, and a reliable sleeping bag.
  3. Leave no trace — pack out everything you bring.
